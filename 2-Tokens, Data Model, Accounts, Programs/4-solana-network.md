### Solana Network:

**Three types:**

    1. Mainnet → Real users, real SOL
    2. Devnet → Public testing network, resets occasionally, free SOL
    3. Testnet → Validator & protocol testing, unstable


**Good Read:**
***[Clusters and Public RPC Endpoints]***
